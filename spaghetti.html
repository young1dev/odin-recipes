How to Cook Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe gives you a quick and tasty way to make jollof spaghetti â€” a Nigerian fave!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of spaghetti
  • 3 fresh tomatoes or 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt & seasoning cubes
  • Spices (curry, thyme, etc.)

Steps

  1. Break spaghetti in half and parboil for 5 mins, then drain.
  2. Blend or slice tomatoes, onions, and fry in oil.
  3. Add salt, seasoning, and spices. Let it simmer into a sauce.
  4. Pour in water, then add spaghetti and cook till soft and dry.
