How to Cook Beans

Description

This recipe teaches you how to cook tasty and soft beans, great with bread or rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of brown beans
  • 1 medium onion
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes (optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pick and rinse the beans properly.
  2. Put in a pot with enough water and bring to a boil.
  3. Add chopped onions and cook until soft (about 45 mins to 1 hour).
  4. Add salt and seasoning cubes towards the end, then simmer for 5 minutes.
